"Don't Look Down"

  • ← MTA
  • Discover Card →

Question: who wears shiny leather dress shoes anymore?

Answer: old dudes with slicked back hair and a husky voice who don’t know exactly how they got here. You got a problem with that?

Seriously, it’s time to reestablish in people’s minds what made Allen Edmonds great in the first place: they’re not shoes, they’re a status symbol. The epitome of quality and craftsmanship. They’re conquering the American frontier and placing yourself at the top of the tallest mountain.

We have an opportunity here to not only remind old school guys how great they look in that pair of Allen Edmonds. But also to inspire the new generation that status = class and style. Not some programmer in a pair of sneakers.
