Generally speaking, regular people are not financially literate (I know I’m not).

They don’t know how to compare interest rates and pick the best line of credit in order to boost their credit score. If they’re even thinking about their credit score, it’s probably because they’re in financial trouble and are looking for a solution to their problem. Ideally, an immediate and easy solution.

Wouldn’t it be great if some god-like force of good offered you a solution on a platter? 0% interest for a year!
