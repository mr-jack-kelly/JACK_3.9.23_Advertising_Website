This is a portfolio about writing. And bringing things together.

You know the beginning of Forest Gump?

He’s sitting at the bus stop, and there’s this feather floating in the air in front him. It’s meant to symbolize Gump’s life, floating from place to place, because he lives in the moment?

If not, I’m trying to say that I’m a lot like Forest Gump. Minus the brain damage (I think). I’ve done a lot of exploring and a lot of living in the moment — which hasn’t always been easy.

It’s the difference between not knowing where you’re going; and being lost. The hardest lesson I’ve learned is saying to myself: “I’m not lost, I’m here!”.

So here I am. And here is my portfolio.
