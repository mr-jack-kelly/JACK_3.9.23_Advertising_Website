"It's Time to Grow Up"

  • ← Discover Card

I’ve been traveling between New York and Philadelphia to see my girlfriend (long distance is fun) and family. And I’ve taken the Peter Pan bus A LOT.

At first I wasn't happy about cramming myself into the bus; which I didn’t see as particularly dignified.

But I just couldn’t afford paying $160 every couple of weeks to take the Amtrak both ways, which wasn’t all the much faster. I made the practical decision to take the bus; and the savings allowed me to travel more frequently with the same time convenience.

Peter Pan as a practical adult speaks to others in my situation who want to travel on a budget. It’s just as convenient as taking the train or a plane. But with the money you save you can travel more.
